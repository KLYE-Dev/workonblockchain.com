Fees for clients (hiring companies)

Per candidate engagement:

  • 12.5% of the first year’s annual salary*, or
  • 20% of the first year’s annual salary*, if the company does not inform BIOTS Limited of the engagement within seven days of the candidate accepting the job offer**.

*Annual salary in respect of offers of employment. Other engagements shall be the subject of an equivalent fee based on the rate agreed.
**We may, at our discretion, waive this higher fee in favour of the lower fee should the candidate promptly mark the job offer as accepted through the workonblockchain.com platform.

These prices are subject to our Terms of Business for Companies and are liable to change from time to time.
