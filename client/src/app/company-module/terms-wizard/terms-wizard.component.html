We want to keep things easy and transparent! That's why we've created this short summary of the important parts of the Terms and Conditions and the Privacy Policy. Please ensure that you understand what you are agreeing to when you create an account.

Who are we

We are BIOTS Limited. Our company registered address is Broad House, The Broadway, Old Hatfield, Herts, United Kingdom, AL9 5BG.

What do we do

We are a on-line recruitment hirirng platform whose services include identifying work opportunities for people registered on our Candidate database and presenting those Candidates to our Clients.

What happens when you create an account

We will review your account as soon as we can after you submit it to us. We do not accept all applications to join our platform.

With an approved account, you will be able to search through our Candidate database, Approach candidates and send them Engagement offers. Until a Candidate approves the first Approach their identity will not be visible. If a Engagement with a candidate is accepted, then you will be subject to paying our Fees.

You will inform us as soon as practicable and in any event within seven days of the acceptance by a Candidate of your offer of an Engagement and in doing so, shall provide us with such information as we may reasonably require in respect of the Engagement in question.

What happens to your data

We primarily use your data to provide the recruitment service described above. We use several third parties to process data. All information that could identify individuals within the European Union is stored exclusively within the European Union.

What are your rights

At any time, you have the right to request the information that we have on your account, or request that we delete your account permanently by emailing us at privacy@workonblockchain.com. We may ask for additional identification to complete this.
